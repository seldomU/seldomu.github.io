Seldom Tools Editor extensions for Unity

Welcome to Seldom Tools

I am dedicated to the creation of game development tools. After working for ten years as AI and gameplay programmer on multiple game projects, I found myself preferring tool work more and more. Thanks to Unity’s openness I am now able to make tools for a wide audience of professional and aspiring developers. These are my projects:

RelationsInspector

A generic node-based editor. It comes with many customization options, but you can get started by adding just two attributes to your code. You tell it what kinds of relations you are interested in and give it an object to inspect. The RelationsInspector then generates a graph and presents it to you.
